About CREST

Empowering the Next Generation of Cyber Defenders

We are a community of passionate cybersecurity enthusiasts dedicated to learning, competing, and innovating in the ever-evolving world of information security.

Our Journey

The CREST Story

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a small group of security enthusiasts, CREST started as a weekly meetup to discuss the latest vulnerabilities, exploits, and defense strategies. What began in a small room has grown into one of the most active cybersecurity communities.

2022

Growth & Recognition

With over 300 members and multiple CTF competition wins, CREST became recognized as a leading cybersecurity club. We expanded our offerings to include workshops, bug bounty programs, and industry partnerships.

2024

The Future

Today, CREST stands as a beacon for aspiring ethical hackers and security professionals. We continue to push boundaries, mentor newcomers, and contribute to making the digital world a safer place for everyone.

Our Mission

To cultivate a thriving community of cybersecurity professionals by providing hands-on learning experiences, fostering ethical hacking practices, and bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world security challenges.

Our Vision

To be the premier platform where aspiring security professionals transform into industry leaders, contributing to a more secure digital ecosystem through innovation, education, and ethical practices.
