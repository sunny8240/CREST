Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the club?

You can join by filling out the contact form above or attending our next meeting. We welcome students of all skill levels!

When do you meet?

We have weekly meetings every Friday at 5 PM in Room 305. Check our Events page for special workshops and competitions.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We offer training sessions for beginners and advanced workshops for experienced members.

Is there a membership fee?

Membership is free for all students. We occasionally have paid workshops for specialized training and certifications.
